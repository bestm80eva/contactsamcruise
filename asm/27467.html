27467: Ringing phone messages
Used by the routines at 30429, 30675 and 31458. Each entry here consists of three bytes. The first byte is the telephone identifier. The second byte, if non-zero, is the message number of the name that must be given when asked 'WHO'S THERE?'. The third byte is the phone message number.
The first entry is for the phone on the top floor of the hotel. It is activated before a new game starts by the routine at 31458.
